Held oceanfront at the Royal Kona Resort, the Royal Kona Luau – Voyagers of the Pacific – invites you to a magical evening under the stars celebrating Hawaiian culture. Watch traditional island arts & crafts presentations, live musical entertainment and exotic dances. Enjoy a culinary feast that’s fit for royalty as you indulge in a wide variety of delicacies. The final highlight of the evening is the spectacular Polynesian show featuring music and dances from the Hawaiian Islands as well as Tahiti, Samoa and Aotearoa (New Zealand).
